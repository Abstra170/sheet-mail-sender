Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

Sheet Mail Sender accesses Google Sheets and Gmail only for the purpose of sending user-authorized personalized emails from Google Sheets.

Data We Access

  • Google Sheets data selected by the user
  • Gmail drafts selected by the user
  • Email sending permission through the user's Gmail account

How We Use Data

Data is used only to generate and send personalized emails requested by the user. We do not sell, rent, share, or transfer user data to third parties.

Data Storage

Sheet Mail Sender does not store user email content, sheet data, or Gmail data on external servers. Email sending actions are initiated directly by the user inside their Google account.
